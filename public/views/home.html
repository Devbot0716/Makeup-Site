ABOUT ME


Hello and Welcome to my website!
My name is Yulia Wilcox and I'm a professional when comes to makeup and hair styles.


Why did I become a hair and makeup artist?


I started my career 5 years ago in Kyiv, Ukraine.I remember the exact day. It was the day of my prom and I had an appointment with a makeup artist. Everything start out great. I asked her to bring out my eyes and make my lips nude. Unfortunately, she only made things worse. I had light blue eyeshadow and maroon lips. The makeup was horrible! I was so shy to tell her the truth, so I paid her and left quickly. When I got home, my mom was in absolute shock at how awful a job the makeup artist had done. I decided to wash my face and try it myself. After that day, I decided that no girl deserves to ever get a horrible job done on her makeup! That is what inpsired me to become a makeup artist.
